    Our formats

    Vertical Carousel

    Rich media format with a high-impact interactive character that takes up vertical navigation and shows different products or services. Perfect for brands' new product launches and for those who want a user interaction rate at the highest levels.

    Viewability: 90-95% Engagement rate: 20-35% CTR: 1.2-1.5%

    Objective: High memorability Availability: Reservation and Programmatic

    Different combinations are available for the dynamics of interaction: together with you we study tailor-made solutions for your communication needs.

      Product Carousel

      Rich media format with a high impact interactive character that shows the distinctive characteristics of the product, guaranteeing a complete discovery of it. Ideal for promoting products with unique features and stimulating user engagement at 360 degrees.

      Viewability: 80-95% Engagement rate: 23-40% CTR: 1.2-3.8%

      Objective: High memorability Availability: Reservation and Programmatic

      Different combinations are available for the dynamics of interaction: together with you we study tailor-made solutions for your communication needs.

        PlaySunrise

        Outstream video format, full screen mobile, with an interactive character, which combines the memorability of the video with the possibility of adding further information in the branded frame.

        Viewability: 85-90% GRP: 74-79%

        Objective: High memorability Availability: Reservation and Programmatic

        Different combinations are available for the dynamics of interaction: together with you we study tailor-made solutions for your communication needs.
